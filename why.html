7 great reasons why Onondaga is
the right place for you

anchor 1 anchor 2

Great Reason #1

Tuition that makes sense.

Did you know two years at Onondaga costs thousands of dollars less than the first two years at most four-year colleges and universities?

You can afford college.

Grants, scholarships, students loans and/or work study jobs may all be within your reach when you apply EARLY for financial aid. Onondaga also offers a unique scholarship program just for adult students that can cover approximately 25% of tuition costs.

Great Reason #2

Online, Evening, Weekend & Accelerated

Course scheduling options to fit your life.

School. Work. Family. It's a lot to juggle in today's fast-paced world. Onondaga offers convenient scheduling options to help you pursue and earn a degree on your time.

Great Reason #3

We're here to help.

If it's been awhile since you've set foot in a classroom, take advantage of the many FREE resources on campus that can give you the tools you need to be successful - Content Tutoring Center, Math Lab, Nightwriter, Study Skills Center, Writing Skills Center

"I didn't realize the amount of support there is until I actually got on campus. For example, the Writing Center - that was really helpful. I'd do the best I could with a paper and then go to the Writing Center for help fine-tuning it."
- Demitrus White, '12, Hospitality

Great Reason #4

 

Bring your kids!

Onondaga's nationally accredited Children's Learning Center provides high-quality, affordable and accessible child care to the campus and our community.

Onondaga students receive enrollment priority!

"Trying to find daycare and figure out where your children are going to go is hard enough. The fact that the Children's Learning Center was right there on campus was huge - if my kids needed me at any point during class breaks, I was able to visit them and be right there at all times."
- Melinda Jensen '13, Nursing

Great Reason #5

Supporting our veterans.

Onondaga's full-time Office of Veterans Affairs serves nearly 300 student-vets each semester and acts as a hub for VA benefits, general counseling, referral services and more.

We can help you make that transition from military to civilian life.

"By sharing my experiences with other veterans, I feel like I'm not the only one out there. I'm not the only one going through the same struggles and strives."
- Carole Fisher, Human Services

Great Reason #6

One stop. No running around.

Student Central is the hub for current and prospective student services. From admission to advising, financial aid, course registration and more - Student Central is the place to go. If you have any question at all, feel free to call, email or drop by to speak with one of our enrollment specialists. It's is located in the atrium of the Gordon Student Center and there are computers and printers available right next door in the Great Room.

Great Reason #7

 

You can get there from here!

With an education from Onondaga Community College, you have options.

Start here. Finish there.

Transfer to one of more than 80 four-year colleges and universities.

Start here. Finish here.

Earn a bachelor's and master's degree right at Onondaga thanks to partnerships through our regional higher education center.

Your future. Your career.

Onondaga's Career and Applied Learning Center can help you get started in your career through services like job shadowing, employment services, career fairs, and resume & cover letter assistance.
